Selected Customers

Orpak delivers comprehensive solutions to oil companies and commercial fleets, improving profitability and optimizing performance from the forecourt to the head office. Orpak’s field-proven products are installed in more than 40,000 service stations and 7.5 million vehicles across 60 countries. In 2017, Orpak joined the Gilbarco Veeder-Root group.

Orpak's Global Presence

Orpak delivers comprehensive service station management solutions to oil companies and commercial fleets, improving profitability and optimizing performance from the forecourt to the head office. Its solutions and services include: Automatic Vehicle Identification, Homebase Station Management, Outdoor Payment Terminals, Wet Stock Management and more. Orpak’s hardware, software, and services enhance the entire retail fuel network and fleet management value chains, be it the single fuel transaction, retail network optimization or business analytics. Orpak has a 35-year track record of global success and innovation.
