Overview

Orpak delivers comprehensive solutions to oil companies and commercial fleets, improving profitability and optimizing performance from the forecourt to the head office. Using Orpak, our customers can optimize, manage and run their business more efficiently. Be it the single transaction, network optimization or business analytics, our hardware, software and services enhance the entire retail fuel network and fleet management value chains.

We offer systems for:
  • Fuel station automation and convenience store management
  • Homebase station management
  • Automatic vehicle identification & customer loyalty
  • Retail network management
  • Forecourt payment
  • Business insight & optimization

Experience the differenc

Orpak has a well-established track record of success and innovation since 1983. Known for rapid deployment of large end-to-end projects, we are one of the top seven suppliers in the downstream petroleum industry. Our solutions are distributed via an international network of subsidiaries and value-added partners, while our field-proven products are installed in more than 40,000 service stations and 7.5 million vehicles across 60 countries.

Orpak provides you with the best of all worlds: world-class R&D, product design and engineering, global professional customer service, as well as uncompromising quality and value. With a full array of technologically innovative yet cost-effective solutions for oil companies and fleets, Orpak is the smart choice.

Our customer base includes major oil companies such as BP, Shell, Total, Caltex, and Lukoil, as well as leading regional retailers worldwide. Orpak is also a dominant provider of fuel management solutions for commercial fleets, with large fleet customers including Walmart, Sysco Foods, major airports, government fleets, and mining operations.

Orpak provides OEM and Private Label services – from design to manufacturing – to leading brand names in the retail fuel network and fleet management sectors.

Orpak prides itself on its investment in its professional customer success, service and training.

International standards and certifications

Orpak does not compromise in its commitment to compliance with the most stringent global quality assurance, public safety, and environmental standards such as CE, UL, and FCC. All of the Company’s products comply with the newly revised EU Directive 2011/65/EU on Restriction of Hazardous Substances (RoHS 2), and we maintain ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 certifications:

ISO 14001certifies the Orpak environmental management system that reduces energy use and waste, cuts costs by improving efficiency, meets worldwide legal obligations, and prepares us and our customers for the constantly changing business landscape.

OHSAS 18001certifies the Orpak occupational health & safety management best practices management system that improves worksite safety, minimizes risk to employees, and demonstrates corporate diligence and concern with the well-being of its employees.

ISO27001certifies the Orpak Information Technology Security to protect their information in a systematic and cost-effective way, through the adoption of an Information Security Management System (ISMS).
